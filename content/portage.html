Directions to the Portage Office

From I-94, take the Westnedge Exit 76A South. Pass Milham and Romence intersections. The next traffic light is Garden Lane. Turn left. The Creekside Suites office building is on the right, just before the bridge over the creeek. My office is Suite 110, the last one on the end.
