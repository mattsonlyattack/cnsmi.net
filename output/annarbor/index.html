Directions to the Ann Arbor Office

From east side of ann Arbor: Take M-14 west to the downtown Ann Arbor Exit #3. Follow Main Street south to Kingsley and turn right. Follow Kingsley which turns left into 1st street. You will see the McKinley Office Building parking lot on the right almost immediately after turning onto First St. Park in the lot in any vistors spot. Walk up the First Street side of the building until you come to the third door that has a sign "Dr. Ennis Berker" next to it. My office is the first one on the right after you enter the door.

From the west side of Ann Arbor: Take M-14 east to exit #2, Miller Ave. Go east on Miller Ave. Just after you go under a railroad viaduct that says "13 feet", turn left into the parking lot of my office building. Walk up the First Street side (east side) of the building until you come to the third door that has a sign "Dr. Ennis Berker" next to it. My office is the first one on the right after you enter the door.
